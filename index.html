Our STORY

Ashley and Justin first met back in 2014 when Justin moonlit as ​a guest lecturer during her capstone Entrepreneurship class, ​which he completed two years prior at Virginia Commonwealth ​University. Little did they know that this chance encounter ​would kickstart an incredible journey together.


Since then, they’ve been on the move, living up and down the ​East Coast—from Buffalo to sunny Florida. Their shared ​passion for travel took them on a cross-country road trip in an ​RV they renovated themselves. They’ve also explored ​Thailand, Canada, Costa Rica, and soon Europe together. ​Settling back in Richmond where they first met, they’ve built a ​home together with their two dogs Stella and Django.


As our story continues to unfold, we're thrilled to celebrate our ​next chapter surrounded by our loved ones.
