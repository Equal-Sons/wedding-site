Our STORY

Ashley and Justin first met back in 2014 when Justin moonlit as a guest lecturer during her capstone Entrepreneurship class, which he completed two years prior at Virginia Commonwealth University. Little did they know that this chance encounter would kickstart an incredible journey together.


Since then, theyve been on the move, living up and down the East Coastfrom Buffalo to sunny Florida. Their shared passion for travel took them on a cross-country road trip in an RV they renovated themselves. Theyve also explored Thailand, Canada, Costa Rica, and soon Europe together. Settling back in Richmond where they first met, theyve built a home together with their two dogs Stella and Django.


As our story continues to unfold, we're thrilled to celebrate our next chapter surrounded by our loved ones.
